Understanding Users & Proto-persona











User Interviews






5 one-on-one Interviews

Key points:

  • Desire to become more "eco-friendly"
  • Lack of clear guidelines
  • Unsure how to properly dispose of products
  • Difficult to access "green" practices in their area
  • No feedback given on eco-friendly contributions

Synthesizing Research into Affinity Diagram



User Persona


  • Cecelia Greene, a young woman interested in increasing her “Green” habits, needs to be able to easily access information about ”Green” practices, in order to become more environmentally-conscious and earn personal satisfaction.
  • Goals: Wants to help preserve the planet for future generations. Wants to learn better and be better accurately performing recycling practices
  • Needs: To feel that her actions have a positive impact on the environment
  • Opportunities for improvement: Frustrations: Unaware of where to dispose of E-waste, Gets confused by numbers on plastic products, Seeing recycable products in the trash, Lack of accessibility to education about recycling,Lack of clear, step-by-step information about recycling readily available to the public
